## 3. Brand Foundation (Umbrella)

**Name origin:** Astu Neon = ancient name of Ostuni, the "White City" of Puglia. Core motif: the **porta** — door/portal/threshold. "Astu Neon is the door to Puglia. The door to heritage. The door to elegance refined to its purest expression."

**One-liner:** "Astu Neon is a luxury house that invites a select clientele into the world of Puglia through taste, place, and ritual."

**Central idea — the ladder (Taste → Live → Belong):**

| Level | Customer is buying | Offers |
|---|---|---|
| Taste | First entry into the world | Olive oil, honey, gifting, tastings |
| Live | Temporary immersion | Villa stays, harvest weekends, chef-led meals |
| Belong | Status, intimacy, stewardship | Private allocations, named-tree program, invitation-only experiences, collector circle |

**Roadmap:** 1) Prove the land (launch oil with obsessive provenance). 2) Deepen the table (honey, preserves, ceramics). 3) Convert buyers into villa guests. 4) Create belonging (stewardship, collector circle).

**Messaging pillars:** Place · Passage · Ritual · Rarity · Belonging.

**Holding-company narrative (BP Elevator Speeches + brand JSON):** "An ecosystem for resilience and innovation, prompting our clients to dream bigger." Brand values: Innovation, Impact, Inspiration. Themes: dreaming belongs to everyone; "we're here to build a fishing boat, and to build it with 80% Italian hands, codes, and minds"; open door / boundless universe; movement built by dreamers.

✅ **Decided (Dave, Jul 2, 2026):** the **visionary narrative leads** on the astuneon.co holding site — bold, charismatic, "ecosystem for resilience and innovation, prompting our clients to dream bigger." The quiet Puglia-luxury voice belongs to the consumer brands beneath it (myastuneon.com oil, villa, experiences), which the holding site links out to.

## 4. Voice & Copy Rules

- Calm, confident, never loud or salesy. Poetic but grounded. Sensory-driven (taste, touch, light, air). Emphasis on time, slowness, permanence. "Something you enter, not just buy."
- **Words to favor:** quiet, refined, private, intimate, rooted, timeless, selective, living, hosted, entrusted, reserved, seasonal, luminous, enduring.
- **Words to avoid:** tourism, package, Airbnb-like, mass luxury, "VIP", flashy exclusivity.
- CTAs soft, never pushy: "Request access," "Begin your journey," "Request a Stay."
- Door/portal/threshold metaphors consistent across all touchpoints.
